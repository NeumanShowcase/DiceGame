What is this?
This webpage consists of a dicegame in which the player guesses on the sum of the outcome of the first three dice.

How do i win?
If the guessed numeber is equal or below the outcome the player wins this sum multiplied by the value of the last dice.

When is it over?
One game consists of ten dice rolls. After which the player will be able to see the final result.

Why do i have to create a user?
You don't have to but if you do you will be able to compare your own top scores and see the global top scores.
